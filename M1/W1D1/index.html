Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onion, tart
cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become yourfavorire holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g)all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instants yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons sal
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, (scrape rindcut into cube off with a parring knife if desided, thougt it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

1. Make the focaccia dought

In a medium-size mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the doungh ingredients until the flour it's fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bow when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
